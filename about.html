Welcome to 8th NATIONAL ADVENTURE FESTIVAL

The 8th National Adventure Festival is a celebration of courage, teamwork, and exploration. It encourages students to step out of their comfort zones, face thrilling challenges, and embrace leadership through adventure-based experiences. Through physically engaging activities and collaborative tasks, this festival aims to build confidence, resilience, and a strong spirit of unity among the youth.

About Us

Notre Dame College Dhaka Adventure Club

Guided by the spirit of “Adventure with Purpose,” the Ideal Science and Technology Aiming Research Council (ISTARC) takes pride in organizing the 8th National Adventure Festival. Founded by senior teacher Md. Motahar Hossain of Ideal School and College, ISTARC has consistently encouraged students not only in scientific innovation but also in developing life skills through practical and outdoor activities. This national festival stands as a thrilling opportunity for students to discover their inner strength, enhance their teamwork, and develop leadership through a variety of adventure-based programs. ISTARC believes that true education goes beyond classrooms — it involves facing real-life challenges, learning through action, and growing through experiences that test both body and mind. From rope climbing and trekking to problem-solving tasks, survival games, and strategy-building group missions — this festival fosters resilience, unity, and courage in a vibrant and safe environment. Over the years, ISTARC has become a national symbol of excellence in both science and adventure education. As an esteemed member of MILSET ASIA and a recognized organization under the Ministry of Education, Bangladesh, ISTARC’s commitment to nurturing future-ready youth has gained international respect. With each passing year, our festivals have evolved into powerful platforms that bring together bright, bold, and enthusiastic minds from all over the country. Celebrating its 23rd year of establishment, ISTARC is proud to mark a new milestone through this adventurous chapter. The 8th National Adventure Festival is not just an event — it’s a movement to shape capable, confident, and compassionate leaders of tomorrow. We are grateful to everyone who joins us in this mission and hope to continue this legacy of inspiration for generations to come.
