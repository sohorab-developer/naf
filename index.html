Background of Adventure Festival

8TH NATIONAL ADVENTURE FESTIVAL 2024

The National Adventure Festival has been a dynamic platform for youth to explore beyond the boundaries of classrooms and textbooks. It encourages students to step out of their comfort zones, embrace challenges, and develop leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. This event is not just about thrill — it's about discovering one's true potential through experiences that blend fun, education, and physical activities. Over the years, the festival has played a vital role in building confidence and inspiring a sense of courage, discipline, and responsibility among the young generation.

About Our Adventure Festival

The Adventure Festival aims to create unforgettable memories by bringing together students from diverse institutions under one spirit of exploration and camaraderie. With a blend of outdoor tasks, strategic games, and interactive activities, this event nurtures physical agility, mental strength, and social connection. Recognized nationally, the festival continues to evolve with greater participation, innovative ideas, and impactful outcomes — shaping the youth into confident and conscious citizens of tomorrow.

Event Description

After a year of eager anticipation, the time for adventure has finally arrived. Following the resounding success of the 7th edition, which set new standards of excellence, the highly awaited event is making its grand return. The Notre Dame Outward Bound Adventure Club (OBAC) is thrilled to present the 8th edition of the National Adventure Festival (NAF), promising a wave of nostalgia, excitement, and unforgettable experiences that will linger long after the event. Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled journey with unique solo and team challenges that will ignite your inner adventurer.


The 8th National Adventure Festival will take place on May 16th and 17th, 2025, at the Notre Dame College campus. This time NAF will completely providing more exciting and flagship games like 6 Legs, Outrush, DND and so on. Keep your eyes open to watch the legacy.
