Background of Adventure Festival

8TH NATIONAL ADVENTURE FESTIVAL 2025

The National Adventure Festival serves as an exciting platform for young people to venture beyond the confines of classrooms and textbooks. It motivates students to step outside their comfort zones, tackle challenges head-on, and cultivate essential skills in leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving. This event is not solely about excitement; it's about uncovering one's true potential through experiences that seamlessly blend enjoyment, education, and physical activity. Over the years, the festival has been instrumental in fostering confidence and instilling a sense of bravery, discipline, and responsibility in the younger generation.

About Our Adventure Festival

The Adventure Festival is designed to create lasting memories by uniting students from various institutions under a shared spirit of exploration and friendship. Through a combination of outdoor challenges, strategic games, and engaging activities, this event promotes physical fitness, mental resilience, and social bonds. Recognized on a national level, the festival continues to grow with increased participation, innovative concepts, and meaningful outcomes — shaping the youth into confident and conscientious citizens for the future.

Event Description

After a year of eager anticipation, the time for adventure has finally arrived. Following the resounding success of the 7th edition, which set new standards of excellence, the highly awaited event is making its grand return. The Notre Dame Outward Bound Adventure Club (OBAC) is thrilled to present the 8th edition of the National Adventure Festival (NAF), promising a wave of nostalgia, excitement, and unforgettable experiences that will linger long after the event. Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled journey with unique solo and team challenges that will ignite your inner adventurer.


The 8th National Adventure Festival will take place on May 16th and 17th, 2025, at the Notre Dame College campus. This time NAF will completely providing more exciting and flagship games like 6 Legs, Outrush, DND and so on. Keep your eyes open to watch the legacy.
