Welcome to 8th NATIONAL ADVENTURE FESTIVAL

The 8th National Adventure Festival is a celebration of courage, teamwork, and exploration. It encourages students to step out of their comfort zones, face thrilling challenges, and embrace leadership through adventure-based experiences. Through physically engaging activities and collaborative tasks, this festival aims to build confidence, resilience, and a strong spirit of unity among the youth.
