Obstacle Ground

Obstacle Ground is an event of short timespan but promises to give plenty of elation. This time around we are bringing a solo version of it. This bewildering event will unfold the swiftness inside you and test your adaptation skills. So, Let's take a look how this event will get conducted.
bbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb Basically, Obstacle Ground game is all about testing one’s physical and mental limits by navigating through a course filled with different obstacles that require agility, strength, balance, and coordination to overcome. Obstacle Ground game requires a high level of physical fitness and coordination. Players must be able to climb, jump, crawl, and balance through various obstacles while maintaining their speed and momentum. The game also requires mental toughness and focus, as the players must be able to quickly adapt to unexpected obstacles and make split-second decisions.

Logic Puzzle

"Puzzles are the embodiment of curiosity." - Walter Bishop. Logic puzzles can be both challenging and rewarding, providing a sense of accomplishment to those who are keen to seek the answers. It requires you to use critical thinking, deductive reasoning, and creative problem-solving to arrive at the correct solution. Put your curious mind to work and tear down the earth to unveil the truth hidden within the riddle. Conquer the game of mind and dive into the adventure of unlocking the mystery.

Category :
  • Junior: (J) Class 6-8
  • Senior: (S) Class 9-12
Rules
  1. A puzzle is solved by coloring squares.
  2. Numbers are clues that show how many squares you have to paint.
  3. Every number represents a sequence
  4. If there is more than one number, there must be at least one blank square between each sequence.
  5. Order is important too. The order for the colored squares is the same as the order in which the numbers appear.
  6. Mark the squares that you know are blank with crosses
Prize Details:
  1. Certificate of Excellence, Crest for the top 3 of each category.
  2. All the participants will receive a Certificate of Participation

Hidden Object

Unlock the mystery, sharpen your eyes, and join the quest to find what's hidden in plain sight! It can be frustrating when you can't seem to locate what you need, even if it's right in front of you. This puzzling aspect of the human mind is a test of concentration and perception. Put your brain to the test and see how quickly you can spot the hidden objects within.

Category :
  1. Junior: Class 6-8
  2. Senior: Class 9-12
Rules and Regulations
  1. This is a detective based indoor event where participants have to find and point out the hidden stuffs in the paper.
  2. The participants will be provided with a tricky picture full of different objects.
  3. If two or more participants get same points then they will be awarded based on who submitted the paper quickly.
  4. The first one to complete his/her task within the shortest time will be declared champion & the second & third one will be declared as first & second runner-ups.
  5. Necessary items will be provided by the authority, but the participants are requested to bring their own pencil, pen etc. but mobile phones are not allowed during the contest.
  6. If any participant is caught adopting unfair means, then the authority might expel him/her silently.
Prize Details:
  • Certificate of Excellence, Crest for the top 3 of each category.
  • All the participants will receive a Certificate of Participation.

In spite of being a small country, Bangladesh has numerous important and magnificent places. Do you know your country well? Would you like to figure out their location scattered around the map? Want to experience how it feels like to be a tour guide? If the answers are yes, then let's jump into the map of Bangladesh with our vintage event "pinpoint on map."

Lensation

Photography is a divine form of art which allows you to define your innerself in the form of still moments. A photo can tell a story words can never express, a photo can take you to a different dimension. There are numerous types of photography each defining different parts of the complex reality we are living in. If you're someone who likes to express yourself in the form of still pictures, We are delighted to launch our flagship event "Lensation". It's the perfect time to show your love towards moment capturing, sky is the limit!

Themes
  • Nature & Wildlife Photography
  • Lifestyle Photography
  • Street Photography
  • Travel Photography
  • Black & White Photography
  • Sports Photography
  • Plane Spotting
Categories :
  1. Junior : (6-10)
    • Digital Camera Photography
    • Mobile Photography
  2. Senior : (11-12) (Including HSC Batch-22)
    • Digital Camera Photography
    • Mobile Photography
Rules and Regulations
  1. One participant can submit a maximum number of 5 photos.
  2. Images collected from the internet or copyrighted (by somebody other than the entrant) cannot be submitted.
  3. The original images should be at least 3 MB in size as selected images will be printed and exhibited. But not more than 8 MB in size.
  4. The Height of the Photo must be at least 3000 pixels (applicable for only Category A)
  5. Only JPG or JPEG versions of the photos are acceptable
  6. Any kind of Border or Watermarks are not acceptable
  7. B/W photos are acceptable too.
  8. Submission procedure and the deadline for submission will be provided later on.
Judging Criteria
We will be accompanied by some high-profile Photographers who have taken the travel photography genre to a different level in our country. All the details about the judges will be posted very soon.

Your photograph will be judged on :
  • Creativity expressed
  • Originality
  • Photographic ability
Prize Details:
  1. For each category, winners (First, Second and Third) will get a crest and certificate of excellence and frame.
  2. All the participants will get a Certificate of participation and photo frame(s).
