Bingo

It's time to reveal one of the flagship events of the 6th National Adventure Festival and that's the most thrilling "Bingo" "Don't overlook what's right in front of you, sometimes the most precious treasures are hidden in plain sight"- and this is exactly what our event is. After receiving a huge response to this extraordinary event in the 5th NAF, we have decided to host it this year too. Join others in an adventure to discover the hidden treasures of our beautiful Notre Dame College. Run through each and every corner, find the stuff and triumph over others in this sensational joyride while shouting "BINGO!!" So embark on a journey of discovery and let exploration be your guide. Believe me, you won't want to miss this one!

Eligible Category: Combined(Junior + Senior)
Event type: Trio (Team of Three)
Rules:
  1. Participants will be given a template that consists of particular objects which they will have to find around the college campus and take pictures of.
  2. Participants will have a time limit within which all the members of the team have to work together in order to find as many items as listed in the template.
  3. After the time limit, participants will have to come back with the pictures of the objects in the template along with marking off the objects in the template.
  4. Team which will find all of the objects/most of the objects in the least time possible within the time limit will be the winners.
  5. Senior and Junior can participate together in the same team.
Prize Details:
  • Certificate of Excellence and Crests for the top 3 teams
  • All the participants will receive a Certificate of Participation.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a game that we’ve all played in our school days. This is a short-timed game but it promises to give a lot of excitement. Basically one has to connect a line of three crosses/circles to win the game. The line can be cross-connected, vertically connected or horizontally connected. There will be a total of 9 boxes to put your circles/crosses. Instead of circles and crosses, we’ll be playing this game with training cones of different colors. Most importantly, this game will not be played with a pen and paper. You’ve to work for it by hitting the ground running!

One team consists of 3 players(Cross-institution is allowed) and the players have to assemble behind a starting line before the start of a game. A box will accommodate 9 small boxes to put those cones in. That box will be set a bit far (100m) away from the starting point. The cones will be set on either side of the box. Whenever the whistle/indication is given, participants have to run the length and put a particular color of cone into a box. When one contestant is done putting his/her cone in a box he/she will come back to the starting point as fast as possible and tag one of his/her teammate to enable that teammate to start running. The team managing to put 3 cones first in a line wins the game. There’s a simple yet key rule to this game, you can’t change the position of the cone once you’ve put it in a box. Also you can’t afford to change the position of a cone of the opposition.

There’ll be different segments for males and females for this intriguing game. So, Bring up your intensity game to the top, contestants!
Category :
  1. Boys (Class 6-12)
  2. Girls (Class 6-12)
Prize Details:
  1. Crest and certificate of Excellence for the top 2 teams for each category.
  2. All the participants will receive a certificate of participation.
Note:
  • Decision of the authority is final
  • Authority has the right to change any decision
  • Any argument with the judge or authority will lead to penalization.

Wall Magazine

A Wall Magazine serves the purpose of providing information and enlighten everyone on a certain topic in the most enthusiastic way possible. This allows the topic of choice to be disclosed by the presenter in the most artistic and innovative way possible.

Category:
  1. Junior: Class 6-8
  2. Senior: Class 9-12
Themes:
  • Ways of adventuring.
    Adventure, an eight lettered word containing a vast amount of ways to execute it. What might be those way of adventure?
  • Summit Seekers.
    The world is full of adventurous people who have shown their ability to conquer the peak in various fields. Who is your favourite among all these people?
  • Thrilling Points of Bangladesh.
    Inspite of being a small country, Bangladesh is filled with both natural and man-made points of adventure. What are the places that come to your mind?
Rules and Regulations:
  1. Every team must consist of no more or less than three members (3).
  2. Magazines containing any kind of inappropriate message or provoking hatred will be disqualified immediately.
  3. Every team should bring their complete Wall Magazine. The authority won't provide any stuff related to magazine or anything.
  4. Team which will find all of the objects/most of the objects in the least time possible within the time limit will be the winners.
  5. Senior and Junior can participate together in the same team.
Prize Details:
  1. Certificate of Excellence, Crest for the top 3 teams of each category.
  2. All the participants will receive a Certificate of Participation.
